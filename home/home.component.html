About Us

Meeting room booking system that allows resource managers and employees to book meeting rooms and track their use

Welcome to Alloq-@, an innovative meeting room reservation and cost allocation system that streamlines the process of booking meeting rooms and allocating costs. Our automated system eliminates the need for manual processes and reduces the risk of errors, allowing for a more efficient and cost-effective approach to managing meeting rooms. With Alloq-@, you can easily book meeting rooms, manage your schedule, and allocate costs based on usage. Our user-friendly interface and advanced features make it easy to use and customize to your specific needs. Whether you're a small business or a large corporation, Alloq-@ can help you save time and money while improving your meeting room management process.
